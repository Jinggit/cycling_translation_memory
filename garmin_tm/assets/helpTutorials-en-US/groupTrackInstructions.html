See your Connections' Positions

GroupTrack displays the locations of your connections on your Garmin device.

To be visible on your GroupTrack-compatible Garmin device, the members of your group must:

  • Be your connection

  • Pair with their smartphone

  • Start a LiveTrack session and turn on GroupTrack

  • Be within range (16km or 10mi)

Then saddle up, partner. You're ready to roll.
