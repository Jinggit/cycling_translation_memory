Off

  • Anyone can view your full name.
  • Logged-in Strava athletes can follow you.
  • Anyone can see your photos.
  • Logged-in Strava athletes can see and download your activities on your Strava profile.

On

  • Your name will be anonymized (e.g. Chris H.) to all logged out athletes.
  • Only Strava athletes that you approve can follow you.
  • Only Strava athletes that you approve can see your photos.
  • Only approved followers can see and download your activities on your Strava profile. Learn more

Further customize your privacy at https://www.strava.com/settings/privacy to:

  • Hide your home, office, or any other locations by creating Privacy Zones.
  • Block individual athletes from following you.

You can make individual activities private by using the "Edit Activity" option. These activities won't be shown to other people, and are not eligible for leaderboards and other public recognition.

For additional questions about Privacy, please contact our Support team from within the app's settings.
