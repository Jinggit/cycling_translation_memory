Enhanced privacy controls

Off

  • Your name will be anonymized (e.g. Chris H.) to all logged out athletes.
  • Logged-in Strava athletes can follow you.
  • Logged-in Strava athletes see your activities on your Strava profile.

On

  • Your name will be anonymized (e.g. Chris H.) to all logged out and in athletes.
  • Only Strava athletes that you approve can follow you.
  • Only approved followers can see your activities on your Strava profile.
